Scholarships Opportunity

We believe that access to education should be available to everyone. Our scholarships aim to support passionate learners who want to begin their journey in Quality Assurance and Software Testing.

Submit Application ➜ Be Contacted ➜ Interview ➜ Announcement of Awarded Scholarships

Eligibility Requirements

  • Be currently enrolled in a technical or academic program.
  • Have basic knowledge of programming or databases.
  • Be involved in a community, student group, or local project.
  • Demonstrate motivation to grow in the field of QA or Software Testing.
  • Be available to complete the full 8-month bootcamp.
  • Have access to a computer and stable internet connection.
  • Commit to participate actively in all sessions and group activities.
