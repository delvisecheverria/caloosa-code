Caloosa Code is an initiative born from the SWFL Tech Night community and led by Delvis Echeverria, a technology professional with 15+ years of experience in software development, specializing in QA and testing. The name Caloosa pays tribute to the Caloosa tribe, an indigenous people of Southwest Florida known for their strength and strong sense of community. In that same spirit, Caloosa Code is rooted locally but built to be global, with the mission of preparing talent not just for opportunities in Southwest Florida but for the global tech market.
