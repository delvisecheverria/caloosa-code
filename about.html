About Caloosa Code

Caloosa Code is an initiative born from the SWFL Tech Night community and led by Delvis Echeverria, a technology professional with 15+ years of experience in software development, specializing in QA and testing.

Delvis has a deep passion for teaching and mentorship, which inspired him to create a program that helps others gain the skills and confidence to grow in the tech industry.

This initiative is designed for anyone interested in entering the software development field â€” whether they are career changers, recent graduates, or tech students looking for their first professional experience. Through a mix of theory and hands-on learning, participants cover software testing fundamentals, Manual Test, automation, APIs, and performance testing, preparing them for real-world work environments.

The name Caloosa pays tribute to the Caloosa tribe, an indigenous people of Southwest Florida known for their strength and strong sense of community.

In that same spirit, Caloosa Code is rooted locally but built to be global, with the mission of preparing talent not just for opportunities in Southwest Florida but for the global tech market.
